Access Guidance

Complete Access With Your Service Provider

Your ServiceNow environment may still be in onboarding, or your access details may still need to be issued by Bynet or your internal administrator. Use the guidance below instead of landing on a missing or inactive tenant URL.

What usually happens next

In marketplace-based enterprise onboarding, users are often asked to wait for final tenant provisioning, user activation, or access instructions before signing in directly to the ServiceNow instance.

  • Complete the service registration or procurement flow with your provider.
  • Wait for your confirmation email with the exact ServiceNow tenant hostname.
  • Use the issued tenant URL and credentials only after access is approved.
If you are unsure whether your environment is active yet, the safest next step is to contact Bynet or your assigned implementation team rather than retrying random subdomains.
