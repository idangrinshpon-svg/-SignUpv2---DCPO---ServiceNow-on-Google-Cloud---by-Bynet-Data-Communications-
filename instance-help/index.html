Find Your ServiceNow Instance

Use the exact ServiceNow tenant hostname assigned to your environment. The login page expects only the subdomain portion.

  • If your full URL is `https://acme.service-now.com`, enter `acme`.
  • Use the hostname from your welcome email, implementation document, or IT administrator.
  • Do not assume your company name is automatically the ServiceNow instance name.
Example: if you enter `bynet`, the site will try `https://bynet.service-now.com/login.do`. If that hostname does not exist, the browser will show a reachability error.
