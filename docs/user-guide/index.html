How customers sign up, sign in, and check offer status

This guide is for customers using the Bynet ServiceNow on Google Cloud experience. Use it when you are following the Marketplace signup or login flow, or when you need to check whether a private offer is scheduled, active, approved, or rejected.

Signup flow

Use the signup page when Google Cloud Marketplace sends you into the registration flow.

  • Open Signup from the site.
  • If Google verification is present, the page shows your Marketplace identity details.
  • Complete the form with your company and contact information.
  • If your offer is automatic approval, the page shows the scheduled start time and account state.

Login flow

Use the login page when you already have a ServiceNow instance and need to continue into your tenant.

  • Open Login.
  • Enter the exact ServiceNow instance subdomain, such as acme.
  • Do not enter only the company name unless that is your actual tenant hostname.
  • If you are unsure, open Instance Help.

What the status pages mean

Scheduled The offer was accepted, but it starts in the future. For automatic offers, customer account approval must still happen before the start time.
Pending A manual entitlement or plan-change request is waiting for an admin to approve it.
Approved The approval step is complete. The record can move forward in the Marketplace lifecycle.
Rejected The request was not approved in time or was explicitly rejected by the approver.
