Privacy Policy

This page explains how contact and marketplace-related information is handled for the DCPO ServiceNow on Google Cloud experience by Bynet Data Communications.

Information Collected

  • Business contact details you submit in the signup form.
  • Google Cloud Marketplace identifiers passed during marketplace flows.
  • Basic technical request data needed to secure and operate the service.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to validate marketplace flows, respond to sales inquiries, coordinate onboarding, and support ServiceNow-related commercial and operational processes.

Retention And Access

Information should be retained only as needed for customer engagement, onboarding, support, security, and legal obligations. Access should be limited to authorized personnel.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact Bynet support and sales.
